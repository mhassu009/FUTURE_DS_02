# FUTURE_DS_02
​FUTURE_DS_02: Customer Retention & Churn Analysis
📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on Customer Relationship Management (CRM) analytics. I analyzed customer behavior patterns to identify why customers leave (Churn), how long they stay (Retention), and their total financial value to the company (CLV).
📊 Key Insights & Findings
Churn Pattern Recognition: Identified key stages where customer attrition is highest and the primary drivers behind it.
Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): Calculated the long-term revenue potential of different customer segments to prioritize marketing efforts.
Retention Analysis: Evaluated the effectiveness of current customer loyalty and developed strategies to improve long-term engagement.
🛠️ Tools Used
Microsoft Excel: Advanced Pivot Tables, Cohort Analysis, and CLV Data Modeling.
Data Visualization: Built dashboards to track retention rates and churn probability.
Gemini 3.1: Used for identifying statistical correlations and generating predictive insights from the dataset.
